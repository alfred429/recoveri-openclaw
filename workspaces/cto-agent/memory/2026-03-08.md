## Etsy Digital Products Pricing Strategy Discussion

**Time:** 12:08 UTC
**Topic:** Etsy digital products pricing strategy

**Key Points:**
- Boss asked for pricing strategy recommendations for Etsy digital products
- Need to consider Etsy's marketplace dynamics, competition, and digital product economics
- Digital products have near-zero marginal costs but require value-based pricing
- Etsy charges listing fees (20¢ per item) and transaction fees (6.5% + payment processing)

**Research Findings:**
- Etsy fee structure for 2026: $0.20 listing fee + 6.5% transaction fee + payment processing (approx 3% + $0.25)
- Typical digital product price ranges:
  - Single printables: $1.99-$3.99
  - Planner packs (30 pages): $7-$15
  - Lightroom presets: $4-$12 (single), $12-$40 (packs)
  - SVG files: $2-$6 (single), $8-$25 (bundles)
  - E-books/guides: $7-$29
  - Brand/business kits: $25-$120
- Successful sellers use tiered licensing (personal/commercial/extended)
- Bundles outperform discounts for revenue generation
- Psychological pricing ($7.99 vs $8) works well on Etsy
