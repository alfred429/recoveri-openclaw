### 1. Executive Summary

RecoveriStudio will launch an Etsy shop selling AI-generated digital productivity tools (planners, frameworks, systems, templates) targeting solopreneurs and freelancers seeking passive income systems. Leveraging CRON_FREE agents and zero-inventory digital delivery, this venture capitalises on the emerging AI productivity niche underserved by current top sellers. Year 1 investment of £314 against conservative revenue projection of £50,000 delivers **15,872% ROI** with payback in under 1 month, representing a low-risk, high-upside opportunity aligned with Recoveri's strategic pillars.

**Growth Trend:** Expanding—Etsy buyers +2% YoY (96.6M total), digital passive income boom post-AI adoption, AI prompts/views at 71k monthly and rising rapidly.
