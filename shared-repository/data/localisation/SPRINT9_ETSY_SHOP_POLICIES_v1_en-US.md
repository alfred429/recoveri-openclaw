## Refund Policy

Etsy does not allow sellers to set formal return policies on digital listings. Refunds are at the seller’s discretion. This is what customers see:

### Recommended Shop Policy Text:

**Digital Downloads — Refund Position**

Due to the instant-access nature of digital products, we do not offer automatic refunds on downloads. However, your satisfaction is important to us.

If you experience any of the following, please message us and we’ll help:
- Technical issues preventing access to your files
- Files that don’t match the listing description
- Incomplete or corrupted downloads
- Accidental duplicate purchases

We respond to all messages within 2 hours and will work with you to find a solution. Most issues are resolved in a single interaction.

If we can’t resolve your issue technically, we’ll review refund requests on a case-by-case basis.

### Internal Logic (not customer-facing):
- Refunds are Boss-only decisions (CS Framework v2, Skill 132)
- CS agents use Template C1 (troubleshoot first, never promise)
- Escalation to Boss via Telegram for all refund requests
- Etsy Purchase Protection covers up to $250 if the item doesn’t match the description
- Dispute window: customer can open a case if no response within 48 hours

## Delivery Method

### Recommended Shop Policy Text:

**Instant Digital Download**

All products are delivered as instant digital downloads through Etsy. After purchase:

1.  You’ll receive a confirmation email with a download link
2.  Your files are also available in your Etsy account under Purchases
3.  Download links are valid for 30 days from purchase
4.  Most products are delivered as a ZIP file containing all included formats

No physical items will be shipped. Delivery is immediate upon payment processing (PayPal payments may take a few extra minutes).

### File Format Information (include per listing):
- Clearly state all file formats included (PDF, XLSX, Google Docs link, etc.)
- State compatible software (e.g., “Works with Microsoft Excel 2016+, Google Sheets, Numbers”)
- State compatible devices (e.g., “Windows, Mac, iOS, Android”)

## Digital Product Terms

### Recommended Shop Policy Text:

**What You’re Purchasing**

When you buy a digital product from RecoveriStudio, you receive a personal-use license to download and use the files included in the listing.

What’s included:
- All files listed in the product description
- Free updates to that product version (if applicable)
- Access to our customer support team

What’s not included:
- Resale or redistribution rights
- Commercial licensing (contact us for business enquiries)
- Custom modifications or design services (we can point you to resources)

All products are tested on multiple devices and operating systems before listing. If you have any compatibility questions, message us before purchasing – we’re happy to help.
