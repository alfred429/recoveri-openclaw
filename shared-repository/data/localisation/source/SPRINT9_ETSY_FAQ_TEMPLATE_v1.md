## Standard FAQ (copy to every listing, adapt bracketed fields per product)

### How do I download my files after purchase?

After payment, Etsy will email you a download link. You can also find your files by going to your Etsy account > Purchases > find your order > click "Download Files." Download links are valid for 30 days. If you paid with PayPal, it may take a few extra minutes to process. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email.

### What file formats are included?

This product includes: [LIST FORMATS — e.g. PDF, XLSX, Google Docs link]. You'll need [LIST SOFTWARE — e.g. Microsoft Excel 2016 or later, Google Sheets, Apple Numbers] to open and use the files. Compatible with [LIST DEVICES — e.g. Windows, Mac, iOS, Android].

### The files are in a ZIP folder — how do I open it?

On Windows: right-click the ZIP file and select "Extract All." On Mac: double-click the ZIP file and it will extract automatically. If you have trouble, try downloading the file again or use a free tool like 7-Zip. Need help? Message us and we'll walk you through it.

### Can I edit this product?

[IF EDITABLE]: Yes! This product is fully editable in [SOFTWARE]. You can change text, colours, layout, and add your own branding. Open the file in [SOFTWARE] and start customising.

[IF NOT EDITABLE]: This is a ready-to-use [FORMAT] file. It's designed to be used as-is for [USE CASE]. If you need an editable version, check our shop for customisable alternatives or message us for recommendations.

### What if I need help or have a problem?

Message us through Etsy. We respond to all messages within 2 hours and most issues are resolved in a single interaction. Whether it's a download problem, compatibility question, or anything else — we're here to help.

### Do you offer refunds on digital products?

Due to the instant-access nature of digital downloads, we review refund requests on a case-by-case basis. If you experience a technical issue or the product doesn't match the listing description, message us and we'll work with you to find a solution. Your satisfaction matters to us.
