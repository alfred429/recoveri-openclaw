## Version 1.0 | 18 March 2026 | Session 11
## Status: DRAFT — Boss to review and decide on visual direction

## Brand Architecture

- **Recoveri** — parent brand. Corporate identity, company values, enterprise.
- **RecoveriStudio** — sub-brand. Customer-facing product experience on Etsy.

All Etsy branding uses RecoveriStudio. The parent brand (Recoveri) appears on the company website (recoveri.io) and corporate docs only.

---

## Positioning

"Premium Digital Operations Toolkit for Growing Businesses"

Not generic templates. Curated, quality-tested business tools backed by UK/European standards and real customer support.

---

## Etsy Shop Banner (760 x 100px minimum, 3360 x 840px recommended)

### Brief for design:

- Clean, minimal, professional
- RecoveriStudio wordmark/logo prominently displayed
- Tagline: "Digital Tools for Growing Businesses" or similar (Boss to approve)
- Colour palette: suggest muted, professional tones — not Etsy-typical bright/craft aesthetic. This is business-facing, not hobby-facing.
- No stock photos of people. Product mockups or clean geometric/abstract design.
- Must read well on mobile (most Etsy traffic is mobile)

### Boss decisions needed:
- [ ] Colour palette preference (dark/light, warm/cool?)
- [ ] Wordmark style (serif/sans-serif, weight?)
- [ ] Tagline choice
- [ ] Any existing brand assets or colours from recoveri.io domain?

## Shop Logo/Icon (500 x 500px)

### Brief for design:

- Works as a small circle crop (Etsy displays it as circle)
- Recognisable at thumbnail size
- Options: "RS" monogram, abstract mark, or stylised "RecoveriStudio" wordmark
- Must work on white AND dark backgrounds
- Professional, not playful

### Boss decisions needed:
- [ ] Monogram, abstract mark, or wordmark?
- [ ] Same colour palette as banner

## Visual Consistency Rules (for all future listings)

Once banner/logo are created, these rules apply to all product listings:

1. **Listing thumbnail images** — consistent template with RecoveriStudio watermark, same colour palette, product mockup on clean background
2. **Listing photos** — minimum 5 per listing: cover mockup, feature breakdown, file format info, compatibility chart, customer support guarantee
3. **Font consistency** — same font family across all listing images
4. **Colour consistency** — same palette. No per-product colour experiments until brand is established.
