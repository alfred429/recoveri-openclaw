## Version 1.0 | 18 March 2026 | Session 11

## Purpose: Proactive message to eliminate Bucket A & B tickets before they happen

---

## What This Is

A proactive message sent to every buyer after purchase. Etsy allows sellers to send one automatic message per order. This message:

1. Thanks the customer
2. Gives them download instructions (prevents Bucket A tickets)
3. Explains what they've bought and how to use it (prevents Bucket B tickets)
4. Offers a support channel (catches anything else early)
5. Plants the seed for future engagement (email list, repeat purchase)

---

## Post-Purchase Message Template

### Configure in: Etsy > Shop Manager > Settings > Info & Appearance > Message to Buyers

```
Thank you for your purchase from RecoveriStudio!

YOUR FILES ARE READY TO DOWNLOAD

1. Check your email for a download link from Etsy
2. Or go to: Your Account > Purchases > find this order > click "Download Files"
3. Download links are valid for 30 days

If you paid with PayPal, it may take a few extra minutes — check your spam folder if needed.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Your download contains: [this will show the listing's file description automatically]

If files are in a ZIP folder:
- Windows: right-click > "Extract All"
- Mac: double-click to auto-extract

NEED HELP?

Reply to this message and we'll get back to you within 2 hours. We're here for download issues, compatibility questions, or anything else.

Enjoy your new tools!
— The RecoveriStudio Team
```

---

## Etsy Configuration Notes

- Etsy's "Message to Buyers" is sent automatically with every order confirmation
- It's the SAME message for all products (you can't customise per listing)
- Keep it generic enough to work for any product type
- Maximum length: 2,000 characters (the template above is ~850)
- The message appears in the buyer's Etsy Messages inbox

## Future Enhancements (not now — after first sales data)

### Phase 2 — Email List Capture (when recoveri.io is live)

Add to the post-purchase message:

```
EXCLUSIVE OFFER

Join our email list for 20% off your next purchase:
recoveri.io/exclusive

You'll also get early access to new products and free resources.
```

This is compliant with Etsy rules — you're not emailing them, you're inviting them to voluntarily sign up via your own website.

### Phase 3 — Follow-Up Download PDF

Include a 1-page PDF in every product ZIP that contains:
- Download/setup instructions with screenshots
- FAQ for that specific product type
- Link to recoveri.io for resources
- Support contact info

This PDF acts as an "in-product" prevention layer — the customer sees it even if they skip the Etsy message.
