## Overview

Three distinct visual concepts for the Finance Hub v2 (Ultimate Budget Planner) Etsy listing. Each concept includes a full 10-image set optimised for Etsy's image gallery, targeting different buyer personas and conversion strategies.

**Etsy Image Specs:** 2000x2000px recommended (1:1 ratio for search thumbnails), up to 10 images per listing, first image = search thumbnail.

## RECOMMENDATION

**Lead with Concept 1 (Dark Dashboard)** for the primary listing because:
1. Matches the existing product aesthetic — authenticity builds trust
2. Dark UI thumbnails stand out in Etsy search (most competitors use light/pastel)
3. Premium positioning supports higher price point
4. Appeals to the power-user segment that leaves detailed reviews (review velocity matters for algorithm)

**Use Concept 3 (Results-Focused) elements** in A/B testing after launch:
- The comparison table (Image 4) is a proven conversion driver
- The "by the numbers" approach works well as a secondary image
- Urgency elements effective for launch pricing period

**Consider Concept 2 (Light & Clean) for the Entry Version:**
- Beginner-friendly aesthetic matches the stripped-down product
- Differentiates entry vs premium visually
- Captures the lifestyle/Pinterest audience that Concept 1 might miss

**Hybrid recommendation:** Primary listing uses Concept 1 images 1-3, Concept 3 images 4 and 6 (comparison table + financial score exclusive), Concept 1 images 5 and 7-8, Concept 3 image 9 (guarantee), and Concept 1 image 10. This combines premium positioning with conversion optimization.
